# Neuroscience of Consciousness — Submission Guidelines

Source: https://academic.oup.com/nc/pages/General_Instructions
Retrieved: 2026-03-04

## Scope
Open access journal on biological basis of consciousness. Welcomes neuroscience, cognitive science, psychology, philosophy, computer science. Topics: consciousness itself, conscious/unconscious interactions, selfhood, emotion, metacognition, intention/volition/agency, altered states, disorders of consciousness, consciousness in infants/animals/machines.

## Streamlined Submission
- Submit via ScholarOne: https://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/ncon
- Editorial office: nc.editorialoffice@theeditorialhub.com
- Any common editable document format accepted (NOT PDF for revised submissions)
- Double line spacing recommended

## Article Types & Limits

| Type | Max words | Max abstract | Max refs |
|------|-----------|-------------|----------|
| Research Article | 9,000 | 250 | n/a |
| Review Article | 10,000 | 250 | n/a |
| Rapid Communication | 3,000 | 250 | 20 |
| Spotlight Commentary | 1,500 | 100 | 10 |

## First Page Requirements
- Title
- All author names
- All author affiliations (format: department, institution, city, state, country)
- Corresponding author mailing address and email

## CRediT Contributor Roles
Required. Use standard CRediT taxonomy. Multiple roles per author allowed.

## Abstract
- Max 250 words
- NO citations
- NO abbreviations

## Figures
- Numbered (Figure 1, Figure 2, etc.)
- Referenced in text
- Common image format (pdf, eps, gif, tif, jpg, png)
- Include legend
- **Alt text REQUIRED**: Include in main manuscript, directly under figure legend, preceded by "Alt text:"
- Initial submission: figures can be inline
- Revision: figures must be separate high-resolution files

## Tables
- Include title
- Footnotes can be added where appropriate
- Avoid overcrowding

## References
- Any readable style at submission (author responsible for accuracy)

## Required Sections (Research/Rapid Communications)
Abstract → Introduction → Methods and Materials → Results → Discussion → Summary/Conclusions

## Required End Matter
1. Acknowledgements (including funding sources)
2. Data Availability Statement (after Acknowledgements)
3. Funding declaration

## Footnotes
**Should be avoided.**

## Non-standard Abbreviations
Define at first occurrence.

## Supplementary Material
- Submit with main manuscript
- Cannot be altered after acceptance
- Name files clearly (e.g., "Supplementary Figure 1")
- Reference in main text

## Suggested Reviewers
- At least 5 names with contact details
- Consider diversity: career stage, gender, geography

## Preprints
- Authors may post preprints
- Must note preprint in submission
- If accepted, update preprint with DOI and acceptance notice:
  "This article has been accepted for publication in [Journal Title] ©: [year] [owner] Published by Oxford University Press. All rights reserved."

## AI/LLM Disclosure
Per OUP policy, disclose AI tool usage in Acknowledgements.

## Open Science Badges
Available for: Open Materials, Open Data, Preregistered, Preregistered + Analysis Plan.

## Key Gotchas
- **Revised submissions MUST be editable format (not PDF)**
- **Figures in revision must be separate high-resolution files**
- **Alt text must be IN the manuscript, not separate**
- **Abstract: no citations, no abbreviations, max 250 words**
- **Footnotes discouraged**
