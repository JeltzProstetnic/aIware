## 1. Dweck's Growth Mindset: **REPLICATION CRISIS**

### Key Finding: Financial Incentive Bias

**Macnamara & Burgoyne (2023)** - *Psychological Bulletin*
- Overall effect: d = 0.05 (nonsignificant after publication bias correction)
- **Bombshell**: Authors with financial incentives to support mindset theory reported significantly larger effects than independent researchers
- High-quality studies only (6 studies, N = 13,571): d = 0.02 (nonsignificant)

**Sisk et al. (2018)** - *Psychological Science*  
- Meta-analysis: r = .09 between mindsets and achievement
- Intervention effect: d = 0.08 (very small)
- Exception: Low-SES students showed 4x larger effects

### Relevance to Your Theory
**Critical insight**: Growth mindset interventions fail precisely because they treat motivation as an add-on rather than integral component of intelligence. Your recursive loop theory explains WHY belief change alone is insufficient—must change the actual loop structure.

## 3. Flynn Effect Reversal: **SMOKING GUN FOR PROCESS VIEW**

### The Evidence

**Bratsberg & Rogeberg (2018)** - *PNAS*
- Norwegian data: IQ rose 99.5 (1962) → 102.3 (1975) → declined to 99.4 (1989)
- **Causation**: Environmental, NOT genetic
- Countries affected: Norway, Finland, Denmark, Estonia, UK, France, Netherlands, Australia

**Gignac & Zajenkowski (2024)** - Austria  
- **Paradox**: IQ scores UP, but g (general intelligence) DOWN (2005-2018)
- Implication: Teaching to test = performance gains WITHOUT genuine intelligence increase

**Platt et al. (2023)** - U.S. SAPA Project
- Declines in verbal reasoning, matrix reasoning, math (2006-2018)
- Exception: Spatial reasoning increased

### Timing
Reversal begins ~2010—smartphone ubiquity

### Relevance to Your Theory
**This is GOLD**. If intelligence were a fixed biological trait, environmental changes wouldn't cause population-level declines. Flynn effect reversal demonstrates intelligence is **a process you do**, not **a capacity you have**. When environment disrupts motivation (smartphones → attention fragmentation), the entire recursive loop degrades.

- Weak incremental validity

### What CHC Excludes
- Motivation
- Metacognition

- Goal-directedness

### Relevance to Your Theory
**Perfect target**. CHC is THE dominant model, and it systematically excludes motivation. This is your core critique. CHC measures what you CAN do (capacity), not what you WILL do (motivated performance). The exclusion of motivation explains CHC's interpretive problems—can't predict real-world performance from capacity alone.
