## 1. INSTITUTION PROFILE

### Name & Identity
- **Official Name**: Institute for Studies of Science (ISoS)
- **German Name**: Institut für Wissenschaftsforschung und -reflexion
- **Status**: Central Academic Institute (Zentrale Wissenschaftliche Einrichtung)
- **Founded**: May 2025 (newly established, very young)
- **Predecessor**: I²SoS (Institute for Interdisciplinary Studies of Science)

### Mission & Research Focus

ISoS integrates **philosophical, historical, and sociological perspectives** on science, medicine, and technology. Core research areas:

1. **Practices of the life sciences**: biology, psychology, neuroscience, medicine
2. **Relations between science and society**: science/values, science/policy, science in application
3. **Interdisciplinary science reflection**: how science works, not just what it discovers

The institute's stated mission is: *"We are the ones who reflect on science"* — studying science as a social, historical, and philosophical phenomenon, not just conducting science itself.

### Scale & Capacity
- **Current membership**: 30+ researchers across 4 faculties
- **Faculties involved**: Medicine, Sociology, Philosophy, History
- **Activities**: Lectures, workshops, doctoral/postdoctoral networking, writing retreats, colloquia
- **Community**: ~40 activities per year across the broader ZiF ecosystem (though ISoS is newer)
