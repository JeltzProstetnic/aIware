## STEP 5: Author Central Setup

1. Go to **author.amazon.com**
2. Sign in with your Amazon/KDP account
3. **Claim your author page** — search for your book by title or ISBN
4. Add:
   - **Bio**: Use the "About the Author" from the book (shortened to ~200 words)
   - **Photo**: Professional headshot
   - **Blog/website link**: Link to Zenodo preprints, your ORCID, and (when created) your Substack
5. **Editorial Reviews section**: Once you have endorsements from researchers (Mediano, Nilsen, etc.), add them here as "Editorial Reviews" — these appear prominently on the product page
